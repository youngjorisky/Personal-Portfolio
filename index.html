ABOUT

I’m a young graduate Electrical Engineer with over 2 years of practical experience in industrial systems and automation. My journey into software began with a curiosity for how technology powers efficiency — leading me to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and now expanding into backend development.

I enjoy solving technical challenges — whether that’s wiring a control panel, designing a responsive web interface, or connecting a PLC to visualize data on an HMI. My goal is to blend engineering principles with modern software development to build solutions that are reliable, efficient, and meaningful.
